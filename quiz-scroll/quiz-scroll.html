THE DEFINERY

Owning objects can be so confusing. You might like something but what do others think of it… Is your object luxury or vulgar? Will you be teased or envied behind your back? Sniggered at or sucked up to? Is it class or crass? Certainty is such a luxury.

Let The Definery help you decide what objects to own.
